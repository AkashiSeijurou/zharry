Canadian Tulip Festival

"Do you love tulips? What about a million of them? That’s how many are planted annually in our nation’s capital, dating back to the postwar years when the Netherlands gave Canada a yearly gift of thousands of tulip bulbs in gratitude for our military’s role in that country’s liberation. The Canadian Tulip Festival returns from May 12 to 22. "
